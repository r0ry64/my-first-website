About Me

My name is Edgar Rory Cobb but everyone calls me Rory. I'm a 24 year old Mexican-Canadian guy from Calgary, Alberta but I currently live in Guelph, Ontario.

I grew up playing old Windows and DOS games because our family had a very old computer when I was a kid. I became interested in creating maps and mods for those old games, and thats where I started learning the basics of programming. I never thought I would persue a career in programming, so I worked in construction for a while and went to school for skilled trades. But after bad experiences, injuries, and other personal issues, I have decided to switch career paths. I've always had an interest and fascination for programming, so I'm glad I'm taking this course :)
